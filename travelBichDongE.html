Tour Travel

BAI DINH PAGODA - HOA LU ANCIENT CAPITAL - MUA CAVE - THAI VI TEMPLE

(Tour code: TNT-PV 01)

Trip Schedule

8:00 AM Guide and driver pick you up and depart to visit Bai Dinh Pagoda
8:30 AM Arrive at Bai Dinh Pagoda. Admire the biggest pagoda in Viet Nam and the centre of Buddhism in Vietnam Northen. Time to visit and admire the beauties of Vietnamese Buddhist Pagoda Style as well as the design and architecture.
0:30 AM Leave Bai Dinh Pagoda to go to Hoa Lu Ancient Capital
0:45 AM Arrive at Hoa Lu – the Ancient Capital of Viet Nam independent Country in 10th Century with the two temples worshiping Kings of Dinh and Early Le Dynasty.A Established by King Dinh Tien Hoang and last for 42 years though 3 first Dynasties Dinh – Early Le – Ly.
2:00 AM Leave Bai Dinh Pagoda to go to Hoa Lu Ancient Capital

  • Entrance fees and sight-seeing tickets.
